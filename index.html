Elementary

Bacong Central School

Bacong Central School is a public elementary school located in Bacong, Negros Oriental, Philippines. It is administered under the Bacong School District, which is part of the Schools Division of Negros Oriental. The school is situated in Roxas Street, West Poblacion, Bacong. Bacong Central School is known for its commitment to education and its participation in initiatives like Brigada Eskwela and the Literacy Enhancement and Numeracy Development Camp (LEND-C).
